## The 15 pages, in order, and what belongs on each

1. **Hero** — the main product name/promise + a hero screenshot in the laptop mockup. This is the
   first image buyers see in the gallery — it has to work hardest.
2. **Everything Included** — a checklist/overview of every deliverable (PDF binder, app access,
   libraries, etc.) so buyers instantly see the full scope.
3. **Product Preview** — a bigger/closer screenshot of the core interface (not cropped tiny).
4. **Benefits** — the *outcomes* the buyer gets (feel in control, doctor-ready data, privacy),
   not just feature names.
5. **Features** — the concrete feature list (what it tracks, what tools are included).
6. **Works With** — compatibility/format info (PDF + web app, any device, Canva/Excel if relevant,
   printable at home).
7. **Perfect For** — who this is for (name the buyer directly).
8. **How It Works** — a simple 1-2-3 step visual (download → open/print → track).
9. **Bonuses** — anything extra beyond the core product (a quick-start guide, printable extras).
10. **FAQ** — 3-4 of the most common buyer questions, answered briefly and legibly.
11. **More Page Previews** — additional screenshots of features not shown yet (a grid or a few
    mini-mockups).
12. **Lifestyle** — a lifestyle-feel image. *Note: we don't have licensed lifestyle photography for
    this product yet — see the master-template README for options.*
13. **Instant Download** — reinforce it's a digital download, no shipping, instant access.
14. **Comparison** — what makes this different from similar products (without naming competitors
    directly or making false claims).
15. **Final CTA** — a closing "get it now" style slide with the offer restated.
